DotNetTextBox控件配置可隐藏菜单

    以下是配置了多个可隐藏菜单工具栏的标准配置文件，也可直接参照system_dntb/menuconifg/default_style3.config文件：

<?xml version="1.0" encoding="utf-8" ?>
<configuration>
<topmenu>dragtoolbar</topmenu>
<topmenu>paragraph</topmenu>
<topmenu>specialtype</topmenu>
<topmenu>dragtoolbarend</topmenu>
<topmenu>dragtoolbar</topmenu>
<!--可隐藏菜单工具栏配置的开始标记,toolbarshow配置节默认为显示工具栏-->
<topmenu>toolbarshow</topmenu>
 <topmenu>justifyleft</topmenu>
<topmenu>justifycenter</topmenu>
<topmenu>justifyright</topmenu>
<topmenu>blank</topmenu>
<topmenu>insertorderedlist</topmenu>
<topmenu>insertunorderedlist</topmenu>
<topmenu>outdent</topmenu>
 <topmenu>indent</topmenu>
<topmenu>blank</topmenu>
<topmenu>undo</topmenu>
<topmenu>redo</topmenu>
<topmenu>blank</topmenu>
<topmenu>delete</topmenu>
<topmenu>cut</topmenu>
<topmenu>search</topmenu>
 <topmenu>copy</topmenu>
<topmenu>paste</topmenu>
<topmenu>printer</topmenu>
<topmenu>toolbarshowend</topmenu>
<!--隐藏工具栏的结束标记,必须与toolbarshow开始标记配对使用,标记区域内的菜单功能将自动进入可隐藏工具栏-->
<topmenu>dragtoolbarend</topmenu>
<topmenu>br</topmenu>
<topmenu>dragtoolbar</topmenu>
 <topmenu>selFont</topmenu>
<topmenu>fontSize</topmenu>
<topmenu>dragtoolbarend</topmenu>
<topmenu>dragtoolbar</topmenu>
<topmenu>bold</topmenu>
<topmenu>italic</topmenu>
<topmenu>underline</topmenu>
 <topmenu>blank</topmenu>
<topmenu>inserthorizontalrule</topmenu>
<topmenu>font</topmenu>
<topmenu>bgcolor</topmenu>
<topmenu>createlink</topmenu>
<topmenu>blank</topmenu>
<topmenu>fullscreen</topmenu>
 <topmenu>table</topmenu>
<topmenu>blank</topmenu>
<!--可隐藏工具栏配置的开始标记,toolbarhide配置节默认为隐藏工具栏-->
<topmenu>toolbarhide</topmenu>
<topmenu>insertfile</topmenu>
<topmenu>insertimage</topmenu>
<topmenu>insertmedia</topmenu>
 <topmenu>inserttemplate</topmenu>
<topmenu>date</topmenu>
<topmenu>emot</topmenu>
<topmenu>toolbarhideend</topmenu>
<!--隐藏工具栏的结束标记,必须与toolbarhide开始标记配对使用,标记区域内的菜单功能将自动进入可隐藏工具栏-->
<topmenu>dragtoolbarend</topmenu>
<statusmenu>dragtoolbar</statusmenu>
<statusmenu>imgsource</statusmenu>
 <statusmenu>imgedit</statusmenu>
<statusmenu>imgpreview</statusmenu>
<statusmenu>plus</statusmenu>
<statusmenu>min</statusmenu>
<statusmenu>dragtoolbarend</statusmenu>
<bottommenu>dragtoolbar</bottommenu>
<!--可隐藏工具栏配置的开始标记,toolbarhide配置节默认为隐藏工具栏-->
<bottommenu>toolbarhide</bottommenu>
 <bottommenu>getpage</bottommenu>
<plugin_bottommenu>openwin,wordfunction,importword.aspx,img/word.gif,400,138</plugin_bottommenu>
<bottommenu>symbol</bottommenu>
<bottommenu>codehighlight</bottommenu>
<bottommenu>help</bottommenu>
<bottommenu>toolbarhideend</bottommenu>
<!--隐藏工具栏的结束标记,必须与toolbarhide开始标记配对使用,标记区域内的菜单功能将自动进入可隐藏工具栏-->
<bottommenu>dragtoolbarend</bottommenu>
 </configuration>

    可隐藏工具栏功能的配置节可以配置多个,一个完整的配置节必须含有toolbarshow及toolbarshowend或toolbarhide及toolbarhideend!
